After showing this installation at the Governor's Island Art Fair in Manhattan, New York, I was approached by Erik Schurink, the Director of Exhibitions at the Long Island Children's Museum. He said that the fantastical nature of my work would be exciting for kids to see. The intention was to help inspire kids and improve general interest of the arts. I happily accepted his offer and we collaborated together to create this solo exhibition.
