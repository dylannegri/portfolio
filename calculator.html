"Creature Calculator" is an early project that I've done while trying to learn the basic principles of JavaScript. It is a quirky calculator that just gives you a few possible combinations of animals that you can combine and browse google images. The you can also manually enter in anything that you which, but the goal of the project was to inspire and encourage exploring unique image searches on the web.
